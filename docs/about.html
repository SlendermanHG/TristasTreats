About the Brand

Custom dessert work with a darker edge and a serious presentation.

The goal is to make the business feel established online in the same way the work already feels established in person.

Visual Identity

Tristas Treats reads best as a moody boutique brand rather than a generic cheerful bakery. The public site leans into midnight plum, ember, bone, and antique gold.

The result feels spooky in spirit, luxe in finish, and still easy to browse.
